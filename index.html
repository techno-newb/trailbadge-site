About the studio

One workshop, many badges.

Trailbadge Co. LLC is an independent maker brand. We build small, well made products under one roof, from software to gear, rather than a single product pretending to be a company.

Each product stands on its own, with its own name, home, and support. Trailbadge is the maker behind them. That structure keeps every product focused while leaving room for the next one.
